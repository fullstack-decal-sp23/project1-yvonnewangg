projects

BetaMax: Integrating Nanotechnology with Regenerative Medicine

Conducted research proposing the use of nanobots in delivering and releasing serotonin to targeted body sites where damaged peripheral nerves were present. Carried out an experiment demonstrating the nature of nerve regeneration on tadpoles using serotonin.

Find Your Way Home: Java

Designed and implemented a 2D tile-based engine that generates pseudo-random worlds. Features include controllable lighting in rooms, obstacles, and a door (the “goal”) leading to the user’s home. Functionalities include saving & loading, and HUD that displays the number of lives left and a tile’s description upon hovering over the tile.
